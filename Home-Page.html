Uriri Island Resort, Migori-Kenya

Welcome to Uriri Island Resort.

Uriri Island Resort is the only resort that is found at the Kenya-Tanzania border. It isconsidered the most beatiful view in Africa, found at the shores of Lake Victoria.

Located at less than 20 minutes drive from Sirare, Kenya-Tanzania border, it is more than a gateway from the noisy crowds of the city.

Our accommodation provide unique experience with genuine hospitality. We have conference facilities that will allow you to hold both high profile meetings and small conferences.

We are located 500 km from Nairobi, Kenya's capital, 300 km from Narok which is the home of Maasai Mara Game Reserve which is popularly known for the wild beasts immigration.
